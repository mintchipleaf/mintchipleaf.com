I'm Mattie Richards and I'm a developer and designer. I want to create, and make things more beautiful. This site is a portfolio of my works that I've published, and is always being updated with new items. Online I usually go by the pseudonym "mint" which is also what I author my games and other media under. The bottom picture to the left is the "weird green squirrel thing" icon that accompanies the name.


Making games is my passion but I always enjoy when I can get my hands dirty with some code. I'm currently using Unity3D and C# (my language of choice) but my earlier games on here were made in JavaScript with HTML5 Canvas (using Splat.) I'm also an active member of my local game dev group GameDevLou. My game names may tip you off that I'm a big fan of stylized titles.


I also listen to a lot of podcasts.
